National Atomic Testing Museum

755 E Flamingo Rd
Las Vegas, NV 89119

The National Atomic Testing Museum showcases the history of Nevada’s nuclear weapons testing site. Visitors will be able to tour the exhibits, experience simulations, and understand more about atomic testing.

Akhob Art Installation

Crystals at CityCenter
3720 S Las Vegas Blvd
Las Vegas, NV 89109

The Akhob Art Installation features a permanent art installation by James Turrell. Hidden within a Louis Vuitton store, it is easily missed, but when visited, it is never forgotten. Schedule a reservation (from four to six people) and immerse yourself in Turrell’s artistry, all for free. Note, those under 15 are not allowed.

  • TripAdvisor

  • Category: Paradise (Free)

Pinball Hall of Fame

1610 E Tropicana Ave
Las Vegas, NV 89119

The Pinball Hall of Fame has over 150 pinball machines available to the public for free. People of all ages, young and old, will enjoy the fun games this venue has to offer. Pinball machines originate from times as old as the 1950s.

The Tank

Golden Nugget
129 Fremont St
Las Vegas, NV 89101

The Tank is a hotel pool that allows you swim with sharks. Includes a waterslide, cabanas and daybeds, and an H2O bar. It is open to people of all age, and kids under 3 are allowed for free, while non-hotel guests pay between $25 to $50.

More Information: Official Website
