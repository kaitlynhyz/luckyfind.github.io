Lake Las Vegas Water Sports

20 Costa Di Lago
Ste 130
Las Vegas, NV 89011

Lake Las Vegas Water Sports offers a large range of activities in the Henderson area such as ice skating, flyboard, wakeboard cables, and boat rides. Enjoy a champagne brunch cruise, a yacht charter, or even stand up paddleboard yoga. Lake Las Vegas also hosts many special events you can check out on their website. Prices vary depending on the activity.

Ethel M Chocolate Factory Store & Cactus Garden

2 Cactus Garden Dr
Henderson, NV 89014
Open Daily, 8AM-8PM

The Ethel M Chocolate Factory over at Henderson is more than just a tour to view the creation of their famous chocolate. This experience also offers a visit to Nevada’s largest cactus garden, a chocolate tasting class, and a photo gallery. Admission is free, so reserve your seat now!

The Tank

Golden Nugget
129 Fremont St
Las Vegas, NV 89101

The Tank is a hotel pool that allows you swim with sharks. Includes a waterslide, cabanas and daybeds, and an H2O bar. It is open to people of all age, and kids under 3 are allowed for free, while non-hotel guests pay between $25 to $50.
